# Business Profile Management

Your business profile is the public-facing identity of your clinic within Genk≈ç. A complete profile builds trust with patients and ensures smooth operations.

---

## Accessing Business Profile

1. Navigate to **Business Profile** in the sidebar (under Management)
2. Or click on the Profile Completion Banner on your dashboard
3. URL: `/admin/business-profile`

---

## Why Your Business Profile Matters

A complete business profile:
- **Builds Patient Trust**: Patients see professional information about your clinic
- **Enables Features**: Some features require organization details
- **Improves Communications**: Your clinic info appears in patient notifications
- **Supports Public Listings**: Your clinic can appear in public directories

## Business Profile Sections

### 1. Organization Information

#### Basic Details
| Field | Description | Required |
|-------|-------------|----------|
| **Organization Name** | Your clinic or practice name | Yes |
| **Legal Name** | Official registered business name | No |
| **Description** | Brief description of your practice | Recommended |

#### Contact Information
| Field | Description | Format |
|-------|-------------|--------|
| **Email** | Primary contact email | email@example.com |
| **Phone** | Main phone number | +1 (555) 123-4567 |
| **Website** | Your practice website | https://example.com |

#### Address Information
| Field | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Street Address** | Building number and street name |
| **Suite/Unit** | Additional address line (optional) |
| **City** | City name |
| **State/Province** | State or province |
| **Postal Code** | ZIP or postal code |
| **Country** | Country (auto-detected) |

#### Logo Upload
- **Supported Formats**: PNG, JPG, WEBP

**To Upload a Logo:**
1. Click the logo upload area or drag and drop your image

## Saving Changes

Changes are saved in two ways:

### Auto-Save
- Some fields save automatically as you type
- Look for the "Saving..." indicator

### Manual Save
- Click the **Save** button in the top-right corner
- Wait for "Saved successfully" confirmation

**Unsaved Changes Alert:**
- If you try to leave with unsaved changes, you'll see a warning
- Choose to save or discard your changes

## Best Practices

### Logo Guidelines
1. Use a high-resolution image for best quality
2. Ensure the logo is legible at small sizes
3. Use a simple, clean design
4. Maintain consistent branding across all materials

### Description Tips
1. Keep it concise (2-3 sentences)
2. Mention your key specialties
3. Include your unique value proposition
4. Use professional language

### Contact Information
1. Use a dedicated business email (not personal)
2. Ensure phone number is answered during business hours
3. Keep website URL current
4. Verify address is accurate for patient navigation

---

## Privacy Considerations

Your business profile information may be visible to:
- Your staff members
- Your patients
- Public directories (if enabled)

**Sensitive Information:**
- Financial details are **never** publicly displayed
- Staff personal information is protected
- Patient counts are private

## Troubleshooting

### Logo Won't Upload
- Check file format (PNG, JPG, WEBP only)
- Reduce file size if over 2MB
- Try a different browser
- Clear browser cache

### Changes Not Saving
- Check internet connection
- Look for error messages
- Refresh the page and try again
- Contact support if the issue persists

### Address Validation Errors
- Ensure address format matches your country
- Check for typos
- Try entering address components separately
